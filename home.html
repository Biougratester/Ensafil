What Is Ensafil and what it is used for?
Ensafil is a tool that that will help you make your decision on what branch of engineering is best for you based on your own preferences.
How to use Ensafil?
Ensafil will ask you to rate subjects that are presented in all branches of the engineering curriculum, and based on your rating Ensafil will show you a list of branches descending form best to worst.
